Technical SEO

Technical SEO That Makes Your Website Easier to Crawl, Understand, and Trust

If Google cannot crawl, interpret, or index your important pages, good design and good copy will not compound. We audit and fix the technical layer so service pages, city pages, guides, and case studies are visible to search engines.

Technical SEO is not a one-time checklist. It is the structural discipline that keeps a growing website from becoming confusing over time.

What we audit and fix

  • Crawl paths, internal links, indexable URLs, robots rules, canonical tags, and sitemap grouping
  • Title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, duplicate pages, and thin page risks
  • Service, LocalBusiness, FAQ, Breadcrumb, Article, and WebSite schema where relevant
  • Redirect planning, broken links, 404 handling, image alt text, and URL hygiene
  • Core Web Vitals, mobile usability, render-blocking assets, and structured data validation

Best fit

  • Sites with many pages but weak or inconsistent organic visibility
  • Businesses expanding into service, industry, location, and blog clusters
  • Teams preparing a redesign and need to avoid losing existing search value

How this connects to the SEO architecture

Every service page is built to sit above relevant industry pages, location pages, guides, and work examples. That gives buyers a clear path through the site and gives search engines a cleaner topical map.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do small business websites need technical SEO?

Yes, especially when they depend on local or service searches. Even a small site needs clean crawl paths, metadata, schema, and indexable service pages.

Will you change website design during a technical SEO project?

Only when the design blocks crawlability, mobile usability, speed, or conversion. Otherwise, we keep the work focused on structure and search health.

Can technical SEO fix low enquiries?

It can fix visibility and crawl problems. If traffic exists but enquiries are weak, we also review copy, proof, CTAs, and conversion flow.

Need this fixed on your website?

Send the current website or the page you want to build. We will map the clearest next step for search, trust, and enquiries.
