Stillness Co.

Redesigning Greater Vancouver's premier wellness brand into a calm mobile-first space and a custom ticketing system.

Wellness & Retreats Greater Vancouver, Canada WordPress & WooCommerce

The Client & Context

Stillness Co. is a wellness sanctuary offering floating sound baths, breathwork, journaling, vocal yoga, and mindfulness practices in the Greater Vancouver area. As Canada's first floating float bath provider, they curate luxury, presence-driven experiences for small, intimate groups.

They offer wellness retreats, floating waitlist signups, memberships, and a physical products shop (affirmation cards, journals, astrology reports).

The Challenge

The client's previous website suffered from heavy cognitive overload. In their own words:

"The current design is very boxed in, the flow is not clear, there are too many things, and too much information. It feels confusing to a user."

Stillness Co. Founder

With 95% of high-value wellness clients browsing on mobile devices, their boxed layout broke on smaller screens, cart systems added excessive friction, and event registrations were split across multiple disconnected services.

The Problem Diagnosis

Our audit revealed critical structural leaks that prevented traffic from booking retreats or buying products:

  • Boxed Grid Navigation: Hidden menus and multi-layered clicks created high cart abandon rates.
  • Slow Performance: Heavy imagery loaded all at once, leading to slow mobile load speeds.
  • Disconnected Bookings: Eventbrite and Calendly links sent users away from the site, losing track of conversion analytics.

Design Principles Applied

To support Stillness Co.'s repositioning into a luxury service, we applied four core design principles:

  • Spacious layouts: We broke the boxed grids to give visual elements room to breathe.
  • Muted elegance: Soft typography, warm tones, and calm, un-cluttered landing pages.
  • Mobile-first: Optimized navigation, large buttons, and touch-friendly gallery scrolls.
  • Quiet luxury: Sensory-focused layout structure that gently invites rather than aggressively sells.

The Implementation Timeline

01

Discovery & Strategy

Conducted WordPress stack audit. Mapped out user journeys from homepage to booking. Standardized page structures for local search visibility.

02

Mobile-First Redesign

Implemented fluid typography and flexible section grids. Migrated existing blog systems and media galleries without losing historical rankings.

03

E-Commerce & Ticketing

Built WooCommerce-native event ticketing. Enabled quantity selectors respecting stock counts, waitlists, and custom branded confirmation emails.

04

Optimization & Launch

Aggressive image compression, database optimization, and Cloudflare CDN caching. Launched with clean 301 redirects and SSL activation.

The Outcome

Solicate successfully turned a cluttered WordPress site into a quiet, luxury digital sanctuary. The business now operates an integrated event management and ticketing platform directly on their own domain, removing third-party fee leakage.
