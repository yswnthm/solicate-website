Shivam Kumar

Building a Netflix-style cinematic portfolio to transition a video editor from Google Drive links to professional studio authority.

Video Editing & Storytelling India Custom HTML & CSS

The Client & Context

Shivam Kumar is a video editor and short-form storyteller who edits high-engagement reels and video projects for multiple creators (including Vihari Vlogs, Glivvy Tech). Shivam came to us with a common freelancer issue: he was pitching to high-value clients using un-organized Google Drive links, which limited his perceived business value.

Shivam needed a premium digital hub to showcase his video pacing, music synchronization, and typography editing styles, enabling him to charge professional creative rates.

The Challenge

Video portfolios demand high performance. Loading multiple high-resolution video files simultaneously causes mobile browsers to freeze. We needed to develop a layout that felt like an immersive, dark, "Netflix-style" interface while ensuring fluid scrolling and rapid asset loads.

"Really loving the website. It feels incredibly premium and professional, giving my edits a proper cinematic stage."

Shivam Kumar

The Problem Diagnosis

Standard editor portfolios struggle to convert leads due to three structural issues:

  • Google Drive Friction: Clients hate downloading heavy files or waiting for Drive folders to render.
  • Un-optimized Video Files: Direct raw video uploads that cause heavy page lag.
  • Lack of Client Testimonials: Review text is disconnected from the actual video work.

The Solicate Solution

We designed and coded a cinematic, black-and-orange themed digital studio platform:

  • Netflix Style UI: A sleek, dark interface with vibrant orange accent triggers.
  • Subtle Animations: Soft transitions that keep focus strictly on the video work.
  • Testimonial Integration: Highlighted reviews from verified creators (Vamshi, Sohail Khan) adjacent to work cards.
  • Subdomain Setup: Deployed on Vercel under a clean address (`shivam.solicate.in`).

The Implementation Timeline

01

Style Confirmation

Coordinated layout styles. Selected the "Dark & Cinematic" direction over minimal design, focusing on video player structures.

02

Custom Code Build

Wrote responsive HTML/CSS structures from scratch to ensure immediate loading speeds on mobile browsers.

03

Video Compression

Compressed MP4/WebM video assets and lazy-loaded player containers, ensuring smooth performance.

04

Domain Launch

Mapped the Vercel deployment to `shivam.solicate.in`, finalized review edits, and closed the handoff.

The Outcome

Shivam received a premium, cinematic digital hub to represent his editing studio. The website resolved his Google Drive pitch issues, providing prospective clients with a professional space to review and book his video packages.
