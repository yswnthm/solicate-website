Sameera Fatima

Structuring a pink and white rose themed digital media kit and link-in-bio hub for a lifestyle influencer.

Lifestyle & Fashion India Custom HTML & CSS

The Client & Context

Sameera Fatima is a lifestyle and fashion influencer who collaborates with major skincare, cosmetic, and apparel brands. She needed a clean, elegant link-in-bio portfolio to replace fragmented social links, making it easier for brand managers to review her metrics and previous work.

Sameera requested an "elegant and classy" aesthetic built around a light pink and white color palette, with rose design accents.

The Challenge

Most link-in-bio layouts look boring and clinical. They lack visual storytelling. Sameera's audience expects premium lifestyle visuals. We needed to design a custom interface that integrated her active social feeds, rates, and brand lists while loading instantly on mobile platforms.

"Thank u soo much for making my Portfolio look this elegant and pretty 💗"

Sameera Fatima

The Problem Diagnosis

Standard link platforms fail to convert brand views because of three core leaks:

  • Generic Templates: Bland layouts that fail to communicate a luxury aesthetic.
  • Slow Feed Speeds: Heavy video content that lags on mobile web browsers.
  • Clunky Media Kits: PDFs that force brand managers to download large attachments.

The Solicate Solution

We engineered a bespoke, responsive web page optimized for mobile link-in-bio systems:

  • Elegant Rose Aesthetic: A custom theme utilizing light pink and white tones.
  • Collaboration Strip: Logo strip featuring major brands (Plum, Lakme, Derma Co).
  • Digital Rate Card: Clear, readable pricing options for brand campaign reviews.
  • Featured Video Grid: Optimized grid showing her top-performing lifestyle reels.

The Implementation Timeline

01

Style Mapping

Coordinated design choices. Selected light pink accents, custom font files, and mapped out campaign features.

02

Frontend Coding

Wrote custom HTML and CSS. Structured a mobile-first column to optimize link-in-bio user flows.

03

Asset Optimization

Compressed lifestyle images and integrated lazy loading for reel thumbnails to ensure fast page loads.

04

Launch & Verification

Deployed to staging. Client approved layout details and shared the design launch across social channels.

The Outcome

Sameera received an elegant, fast-loading digital asset. The website resolved her content fragmentation issues, providing brand managers with a professional, comprehensive space to view her collaborative work.
