Sankho Kun

A storytelling-focused digital archive documenting creative growth and intentional living.

YouTube & Personal Brand India Custom HTML & CSS

The Showcase Concept

Sankho Kun is a content creator and YouTuber focused on minimalist lifestyles and creative growth. This project was developed by Solicate as a spec-to-portfolio case study to demonstrate how a storytelling-focused layout can capture a personal brand's essence better than standard social grids.

The site serves as a live portfolio demo hosted at `sankho.solicate.in`, offering brand managers an organized, visual narrative of his creative history.

The Challenge & Solution

YouTubers often struggle to organize their diverse work (videos, journals, photography, podcasts) into a single, cohesive layout. A basic list of video links looks cluttered and fails to communicate their brand's philosophy. The challenge was building an editorial-style archive that paced his content thoughtfully.

We built a grid system incorporating large whitespace blocks, clean sans-serif typography, and descriptive text sections, allowing the creator's philosophy to take center stage alongside his visual assets.

Design Highlights

Minimalist Aesthetics

Clean layout borders and generous margins to reflect his focus on intentional living.

Storytelling Flow

Integrated narrative blocks that guide visitors through his creative timeline.

Custom Video Cards

Optimized preview cards featuring high-fidelity video thumbnails and direct play links.

Subtle Hover Triggers

Responsive design states that encourage exploration without causing visual fatigue.
