Protik Das

A dark, high-contrast digital showcase for a cinematic video editor featuring motion backgrounds.

Video Editing & Motion India Custom HTML & CSS

The Showcase Concept

Protik Das is a cinematic video editor who requested a high-end portfolio page. This project was developed as a custom design partnership model. During development, the client paused the scope. To preserve our custom engineering work, we migrated the code into a standalone portfolio showcase on `protik.solicate.in`.

This page stands as an active demonstration of our dark mode design capability, incorporating cinematic background animations and bold layout structures.

The Challenge & Solution

Cinematic video work demands a website that feels like a theater. Traditional templates with white backgrounds distract from video editing details. The challenge was building an immersive dark theme that loaded instantly on mobile devices without crashing or lagging.

We solved this by writing vanilla HTML and CSS, wrapping the background video in lightweight loops, and stripping away bloated JavaScript libraries to keep the focus entirely on the video work.

Design Highlights

Cinematic Canvas

A custom dark mode theme that frames and enhances colorful video assets.

Bold Typography

High-contrast serif headings that convey a modern, premium branding style.

Video Background Loop

Compressed video files loop continuously in the background without affecting scroll performance.

Micro-Animations

Subtle transitions on hover states that guide the viewer to contact sections.
