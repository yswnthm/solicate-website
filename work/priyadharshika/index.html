Priyadharshika

Structuring a premium 'Luxe Minimalist' digital presence in warm, earthy tones to showcase UGC video campaigns.

UGC & Content Creation India Custom HTML & CSS

The Client & Context

Priyadharshika is a UGC content creator specializing in beauty, fashion, and lifestyle niches. She came to Solicate as a direct referral from Kajal Goyal. To pitch to high-value brands, she required a professional digital canvas that showcased her visual content and rate cards in a clean, modern layout.

For her creative direction, the client selected a "Luxe Minimalist" aesthetic: warm & earthy tones (cream, beige, soft brown, gold) with elegant serif headings.

The Challenge

The main challenge was momentum. Due to personal commitments, the client faced delays in sending her high-resolution videos and assets. To prevent project stagnation, the Solicate team took a low-friction approach, pulling assets directly from her public social channels and creating a refined copywriting layout to build the first draft preview.

"I saw the whole layout, its really very beautiful and premium to look, every detail is impressive ⭐️ I love your work, its so professional ✨️"

Priyadharshika

The Problem Diagnosis

Standard creator sites fail to hold brand managers' attention due to three common leaks:

  • Poor Mobile Optimization: Complex media files that fail to load correctly on mobile devices.
  • Disorganized Galleries: Video categories (beauty, fashion, lifestyle) are mixed together, confusing visitors.
  • Weak Call-to-Actions: Contact buttons are small, hidden, or broken.

The Solicate Solution

We designed and coded a custom, responsive single-page portfolio focused on visual trust:

  • Warm Earthy Palette: Color styling using creams, beiges, and gold elements.
  • Category-Based Grid: Segmented video sections (Beauty, Fashion, Lifestyle).
  • Copywriting Assets: Tailored bios and taglines to highlight her creative experience.
  • Subdomain Deployment: Launched on a clean, professional address (`dharshi.solicate.in`).

The Implementation Timeline

01

Discovery & Onboarding

Set the layout requirements. Sourced assets directly from her social media to reduce onboarding friction.

02

Development & Mockup

Wrote the responsive HTML/CSS structures. Constructed the grid layout matching her earthy color preferences.

03

Revision & Copywriting

Refined the layout details, adjusting spacing and linking the main CTA directly to her Instagram account.

04

Deployment & Launch

Secured her sub-domain, verified mobile responsiveness, and finalized the handoff with positive client reviews.

The Outcome

Priyadharshika received a premium, mobile-first portfolio to represent her business. Sourcing her as a Kajal Goyal referral verified our client referral network, proving that high satisfaction directly fuels new agency growth.
