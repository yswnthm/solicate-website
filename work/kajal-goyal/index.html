Kajal Goyal

Building a high-performing custom UGC creator portfolio that launched a referral loop.

UGC & Content Creation Ahmedabad, India Custom HTML & CSS

The Client & Context

Kajal Goyal is a prominent UGC (User Generated Content) creator working with leading brands. In the highly competitive UGC space, having a professional portfolio is the key separator between low-paying creator gigs and high-ticket retainer contracts.

Kajal needed a central, premium space that she could send to brand managers to pitch her services and showcase her creative video and photography work.

The Challenge

Most creators use generic link-in-bio templates (Linktree, Carrd) which look identical, load slowly, and feel amateur to premium brand managers. Kajal's goal was absolute distinction — a website that immediately screams high-end aesthetics, loads instantly, and handles video assets natively without lagging.

"The portfolio looks incredibly clean and professional. The animations feel premium without being overwhelming."

Kajal Goyal

The Problem Diagnosis

Standard creator sites fail to convert brand views into paid enquiries because of three flaws:

  • Poor Video Optimization: Un-optimized TikTok/Reel embeds that slow down page speeds.
  • Lack of Brand Proof: Brand collaborations are buried under menus instead of being highlighted upfront.
  • Standard Layout Clutter: Clunky, bloated templates that don't match the creator's personal brand colors.

The Solicate Solution

We designed and coded a custom, lightweight HTML/CSS portfolio tailored to her personal brand identity:

  • Bold Typography: Strong editorial header styling to establish immediate authority.
  • Cinematic Video Reels: Optimized, fast-loading grid layouts for short-form reels.
  • Clean Animations: Micro-transitions that guide visitors from work samples to the contact path.
  • Conversion Focus: Prominent CTAs linking directly to her professional email and WhatsApp.

The Implementation Timeline

01

Aesthetic Alignment

Conducted discovery sessions to align on typography and style directions. Selected a bold, high-contrast, editorial layout concept.

02

Custom Code Build

Wrote clean, vanilla HTML and CSS from scratch to ensure instant loading times and complete control over the layout spacing.

03

Asset Optimization

Compressed and optimized UGC video thumbnails, ensuring zero lag on mobile devices when loading multiple reels.

04

Launch & Testimonial

Deployed to Vercel and secured custom subdomain. Launched with positive feedback shared across LinkedIn and Instagram.

The Outcome

The launch was a major success, resulting in high client satisfaction and generating a direct referral loop. Kajal's website became a cornerstone asset for Solicate, producing three new client conversions through her network.
