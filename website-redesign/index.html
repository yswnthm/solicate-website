Website Redesign That Actually Ranks on Google

Most website redesigns make a business look better but rank no differently. The new design goes live, traffic stays flat, and the investment fades. Solicate redesigns websites differently: we fix the structure for Google before we touch the visual design.

A redesign without SEO structure is a paint job on a broken engine. We fix the engine first — service pages, local signals, page hierarchy, metadata, schema — then apply a premium visual layer that supports the rankings.

What a standard redesign misses

Standard redesign

  • New fonts, colors, and layout
  • Better-looking homepage
  • Same weak service page structure
  • No local SEO signals added
  • No schema markup
  • Same buried contact path
  • Google still cannot rank it

Solicate redesign

  • Page structure rebuilt for search intent
  • Dedicated service and location pages
  • Title tags and headings rewritten
  • LocalBusiness schema with your address
  • Mobile speed optimised
  • WhatsApp and enquiry paths fixed
  • Premium visual design built on a rankable foundation

Who this is for

Businesses with websites that:

  • Look dated or feel unprofessional but do not know where to start
  • Went through a redesign but still get no Google traffic

  • Want one revamp that compounds over time — not a design refresh every two years
