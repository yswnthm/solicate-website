UI/UX Design for Business Websites — Built to Rank and Convert

Good UI/UX is not just how a website looks. It is how clearly a buyer understands what you do, how quickly they find the information they need, and how naturally they reach your contact path. Solicate designs business websites where every visual decision supports the user journey — and the Google ranking.

Most UI/UX agencies design for aesthetics and hand you a Figma file. We design for conversion — then build it in code, optimise it for mobile speed, and launch it with full SEO structure. Design and development in one studio, based in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh.

What our UI/UX design covers

Mobile-first layouts — designed for how buyers in AP and India actually browse: on phone, on slow connections
Hierarchy and readability — headings, spacing, and typography that guide buyers to the right information without effort
Gallery and proof design — portfolio layouts, testimonial sections, and case studies that build trust visually
Enquiry UX — WhatsApp buttons, call CTAs, contact forms, and booking flows placed exactly where buyers look for them
Navigation design — clear, fast navigation so no buyer leaves because they could not find the page they needed
Dark/light design systems — consistent tokens for colors, spacing, and typography across all pages
Performance-conscious design — images, animations, and fonts chosen to keep Core Web Vitals in the green
