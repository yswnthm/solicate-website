Website Design for Photographers — Portfolio Sites That Rank on Google

Most photographer websites are galleries with no SEO. Beautiful images that Google cannot read, no service pages, no city targeting, and a contact form buried at the bottom. Your portfolio is your proof — but it needs structure around it so Google can show it to buyers who are actively searching for a photographer in your city.

What a photographers website needs

Image galleries with descriptive alt text: 'outdoor wedding photography Kakinada' not 'IMG_4521'
Individual portfolio pages with event context, location, and services delivered
Dedicated service pages: wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, corporate events
City-specific landing pages for every city you serve
Mobile-first design — brides browse on phone, your site must load fast
Lazy loading for gallery images so speed stays above 70 on PageSpeed
WhatsApp booking CTA — the fastest path from 'I like this work' to 'I want to hire you'
