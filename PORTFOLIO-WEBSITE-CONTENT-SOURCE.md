## 1. Core Rule

The new Solicate website is not a normal portfolio gallery.

It is an SEO-led agency website built to:

1. Explain a sharp commercial offer.
2. Rank for real buyer questions and niche searches.
3. Prove capability through accurately labelled work.
4. Move visitors toward an audit, WhatsApp conversation, call, or project enquiry.

Every public claim must be traceable to a project record, testimonial, screenshot, analytics record, or live implementation.

Do not present:

- A lead as a completed client.
- A proposal as delivered work.
- A concept build as a real client result.
- A design improvement as an SEO ranking result.
- An estimated outcome as a measured outcome.
- A private project detail without permission.

## 3. Offer Structure

### Primary entry offer: SEO Revamp Audit

Purpose:

- Diagnose why a website is not ranking, communicating clearly, or producing enquiries.
- Separate design problems from structure, speed, content, proof, and conversion problems.
- Give the prospect a prioritized next step.

Audit checks:

- Indexing and crawlability.
- Titles, headings, metadata, and search intent.
- Service and location page gaps.
- Mobile speed and gallery weight.
- Proof placement.
- WhatsApp, call, booking, order, and form paths.
- FAQ and answer-ready content.
- Analytics and enquiry tracking.

Do not call the audit free unless Solicate is actually prepared to deliver it manually at no cost.

### Main offer: SEO-First Website Revamp

Public meaning:

> A structured website rebuild that fixes search clarity, buyer clarity, proof, mobile experience, and enquiry paths before applying the premium visual layer.

Possible scope:

- Discovery and website audit.
- Search-intent sitemap.
- Service and location page planning.
- Content hierarchy.
- Premium visual redesign.
- Mobile-first development.
- Technical SEO foundation.
- Structured data where accurate.
- Gallery and image optimization.
- WhatsApp, phone, form, booking, or order flow.
- Analytics and event tracking.
- Launch QA and handoff.

### Expansion offer: Search Capture Pages

For businesses that need focused pages around:

- A service.
- A city or service area.
- A high-intent buyer question.
- A campaign.
- A specific property, event, package, or category.

### Secondary offer: Catalog-to-WhatsApp Website

For Instagram-first product businesses that need:

- Product categories.
- Mobile product cards.
- Product details.
- WhatsApp order actions.
- Delivery, customization, and payment explanations.

This is a valid capability and active offer. Do not use Customized Gifts as completed proof until that build is delivered.

### Secondary offer: Build Support for Designers

Positioning:

Possible scope:

Level 3 requires analytics, CRM records, or a written client confirmation.
