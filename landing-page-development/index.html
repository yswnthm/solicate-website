Best fit

Frequently Asked Questions

Do landing pages work for organic SEO?

They can, if the page targets a real search intent and has enough useful content. For pure ad campaigns, speed, message match, proof, and CTA clarity matter more.

Can you connect a landing page to ads?

We can prepare the page for campaign traffic with tracking-ready CTAs, fast load speed, and copy that matches the offer.

How many sections should a landing page have?

Enough to make the decision clear. Most strong landing pages need a hero, proof, offer details, benefits, process, objections, FAQs, and final CTA.
