Yes. We can add phone, WhatsApp, form, or booking-system paths depending on the clinic workflow.

Can you create pages for each treatment?

Yes. Treatment pages help patients understand the service and help the website rank for specific local searches.

Do healthcare websites need special trust sections?

Yes. Doctor credentials, clinic photos, reviews, FAQs, timings, maps, and clear treatment explanations matter heavily for patient trust.
