AI Website Builder vs Custom Website: What You Need to Know

AI tools can now generate a website in 60 seconds. But can they generate a website that ranks on Google and actually converts visitors into paying clients?

The AI Illusion

AI website builders are fantastic for prototyping or creating a quick hobby site. However, they fail at the structural level required for a serious business.

  • Generic Copy: AI generates safe, generic text that fails to establish deep trust with your specific local audience.
  • Poor SEO Silos: AI does not know how to map out a multi-city Location structure or Industry-specific service hubs.
  • Code Bloat: Similar to visual page builders, AI builders often output incredibly heavy, unoptimized code.
