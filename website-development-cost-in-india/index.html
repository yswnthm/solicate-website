Website Development Cost in India: A Practical 2026 Pricing Guide

Website pricing in India is confusing because people compare very different scopes as if they are the same thing. A one-page brochure, a proper business website, an SEO-first website, and an ecommerce site are different investments.

The short answer: a serious business website in India usually costs between ₹40,000 and ₹1,50,000. Smaller landing pages can cost less. SEO-first, ecommerce, booking, or custom CMS websites can cost more because the work includes strategy, content, systems, and technical structure.

Why two websites with the same page count can cost differently

A five-page website can be a thin brochure or a serious sales asset. The difference is not only page count. The real cost drivers are:

  • Strategy: mapping services, buyer intent, location targets, proof, and conversion paths before design starts.
  • Content: writing useful service copy, FAQs, case studies, pricing signals, and local relevance instead of generic filler.
  • Design: custom layouts, premium art direction, mobile polish, and brand-specific visual hierarchy.
  • SEO: metadata, headings, schema, internal links, sitemap grouping, canonical tags, and crawlable content.
  • Functionality: CMS editing, ecommerce, booking, forms, WhatsApp, maps, filters, integrations, and tracking.
  • Performance: image optimization, fast rendering, stable layouts, clean CSS, and reduced JavaScript weight.

Cheap website vs serious business website

Low-cost website

  • Generic template and thin pages
  • Same content structure as every competitor
  • No dedicated service or location pages
  • No schema, sitemap planning, or internal link strategy
  • Weak proof and unclear enquiry path

Serious business website

  • Pages mapped to buyer intent and search demand
  • Clear service, proof, location, FAQ, and contact structure
  • Fast mobile experience with crawlable HTML content
  • Metadata, schema, internal links, and sitemap coverage
  • Conversion paths built around calls, WhatsApp, forms, and audits

What Solicate usually recommends

For most Indian service businesses, the best starting point is not the cheapest website. It is a focused business website with strong structure: homepage, 3-6 service pages, proof sections, FAQs, local context, technical SEO, and clear enquiry paths.

If the business already has a weak website, a website redesign or SEO-first revamp may be better than starting from scratch. If the business is new, a business website development project with SEO planning from day one is cleaner.
