Frequently Asked Questions

Is local SEO only for Google Maps?

No. Google Maps is important, but strong local SEO also needs city pages, service pages, reviews, local schema, and consistent business information.

How many location pages should a business create?

Only as many as it can support with useful local content, proof, service relevance, and clear internal links. Thin location pages can hurt more than help.
