Custom Website Cost in India: What You Actually Pay For

When a business owner sees a quote for ₹80,000 and another for ₹8,000, the immediate question is: "Why the massive difference?" The short answer is that you aren't buying the same product. One is a templated brochure; the other is a custom-engineered sales asset.

The breakdown: Where the money goes in a custom build

A true custom website is not just "custom colors." It means the entire architecture is built around your specific business goals, search demand, and buyer psychology.

01

Strategy & Architecture (15-20%)

Before writing a single line of code, an agency maps out the URL structure, sitemaps, conversion paths, and keyword targeting. This ensures you don't have to rebuild the site next year.

02

UX/UI & Copywriting (30-40%)

Designing bespoke layouts that guide the eye to your call-to-action, paired with copy that actually answers your buyers' objections. No lorem ipsum filler.

03

Development & Performance (30-40%)

Writing clean, lightweight code (HTML/CSS/JS) without heavy plugins. This guarantees lightning-fast load times and perfect Core Web Vitals scores—a massive Google ranking factor.

04

Technical SEO & Launch (10-15%)

Implementing JSON-LD schema, canonical tags, optimized metadata, 301 redirects, and connecting Google Search Console properly.

The ROI of going custom

Custom websites cost more upfront, but they generate compounding returns because they actually rank on Google and convert the traffic they receive.
