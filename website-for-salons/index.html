Frequently Asked Questions

Can clients book appointments directly on the website?

Yes. We integrate leading booking systems directly into your layout so clients can choose a stylist, select a service, and book a slot in under 60 seconds without leaving your domain.

Will you show our price list online?

We highly recommend displaying either full pricing or a 'starting from' guide. This builds transparent trust, filters out non-target leads, and saves your reception desk hours of answering basic price inquiries.

How does local search help my salon grow?

Most salon searches are local (e.g. 'hair salon near me' or 'best spa in Hyderabad'). By adding local schema markup and optimizing your site for Google Maps, we make sure your salon ranks at the top of these high-intent local queries.
