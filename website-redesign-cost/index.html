Website Redesign Cost in India: Why You Need an SEO Revamp Instead

Redesigning your website just to make it "look better" is usually a waste of money. If your website isn't ranking on Google and generating leads, changing the colors won't fix it. You need a structural SEO revamp.

Redesign vs Revamp

Visual Redesign

  • Cost: ₹15k - ₹30k
  • Focuses purely on aesthetics.
  • Keeps the same broken site structure.
  • Ignores Google Search Intent.
  • Does not improve lead generation.

SEO-First Revamp

  • Cost: ₹50k - ₹1.5L+
  • Rebuilds the URL hierarchy for ranking.
  • Writes high-intent landing page copy.
  • Injects LocalBusiness and FAQ Schema.
  • Optimizes Core Web Vitals (Speed).
  • Drives actual organic revenue.

What determines the cost of a revamp?

  • Migration Risk: Preserving your existing rankings via careful 301 redirects requires technical expertise.
  • Content Expansion: We often need to write 10-20 new Service and Industry pages that didn't exist before.
  • Technical Overhaul: Stripping out heavy plugins and bloat to achieve lightning-fast loading speeds.

Don't redesign your site until you know what's broken.
