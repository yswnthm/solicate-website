What SEO actually means for a Kakinada business

SEO is not paid advertising. It is the process of making your website appear in Google's organic results when someone searches for your service in Kakinada or the East Godavari district.

When a buyer searches "wedding photographer Kakinada" or "event decorator near me" — Google decides which websites to show first based on signals in the website itself: the page titles, headings, content, schema markup, and local signals like your Google Business Profile and address.

Most small business websites in Kakinada are missing these signals. That is why they do not rank, even when the business is good.

Why your Kakinada website is not ranking

No city intent in titles or headings — Google does not know your business serves Kakinada specifically
Weak or missing service pages — one generic "Services" page instead of individual pages for each service
No LocalBusiness schema — Google cannot confirm your location or service area
No Google Business Profile (or a suspended one) — missing from Google Maps and local pack results
Slow mobile loading — Google penalises sites that load slowly on mobile, which is how most buyers in AP search
No sitemap or canonical tags — Google cannot crawl your site efficiently
