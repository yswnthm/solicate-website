Frequently Asked Questions

Can education websites include admission forms?

Yes. Forms can collect student details, parent details, course interest, location, batch preference, and callback requests.

Can the institute team update announcements?

Yes. We can build update sections through a CMS or maintain them monthly depending on the team workflow.

Do schools and institutes need local SEO?

Yes. Parents and students search heavily by city, area, course, and institution type. Location structure helps capture those searches.
