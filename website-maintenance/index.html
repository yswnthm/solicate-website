Website Maintenance That Keeps Your Site Fast, Current, and Useful

A website that is not maintained slowly becomes less accurate, less secure, slower, and weaker for search. We help businesses keep their core pages, technical SEO, content, backups, and conversion paths healthy after launch.

Maintenance is not just changing text. It protects the website as a business asset.

What maintenance can include

  • Content updates for services, pricing, testimonials, team, case studies, and FAQs
  • Speed checks, image optimization, plugin/theme updates where relevant, and uptime review
  • Backup checks, security hygiene, form testing, WhatsApp link checks, and broken link fixes
  • Monthly SEO hygiene for metadata, sitemap coverage, internal links, and page quality
  • Small conversion improvements based on enquiries, analytics, and business changes

Best fit

  • Businesses that launched a website but do not have an internal web team
  • WordPress or CMS websites that need updates without breaking layout or SEO
  • Growing brands that add new work, services, cities, or content every month

How this connects to the SEO architecture

Every service page is built to sit above relevant industry pages, location pages, guides, and work examples. That gives buyers a clear path through the site and gives search engines a cleaner topical map.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you maintain websites you did not build?

Yes, after an audit. We first check the platform, access, code quality, plugin health, and current SEO risks before taking responsibility.

Can maintenance include SEO updates?

Yes. Maintenance can include metadata fixes, internal links, content updates, sitemap checks, broken link fixes, and new page publishing.

Is maintenance monthly or one-time?

Both are possible. Monthly maintenance is better for active business websites, while one-time support works for urgent fixes or focused updates.
