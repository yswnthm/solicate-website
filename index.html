The Real Problem

Why your website is invisible to Google — even if it looks good

Most visual businesses invest in a better-looking site and still get the same results. The structural leaks remain: weak service pages, missing city intent, slow galleries, buried contact paths, and no clean way to track enquiries.

Common leaks we fix

  • Trendy design but weak service pages.
  • Beautiful homepage but no local SEO.
  • Instagram proof but weak website flow.
  • Gallery pages that load slowly on mobile.
  • No dedicated page for high-intent searches.
  • No clear WhatsApp, call, booking, or form path.
  • No tracking for enquiries.

What we regularly delete

  • Load-screen animations that hurt Core Web Vitals.
  • Vague "We make dreams come true" headlines that confuse search engines.
  • Contact pages hidden behind three layers of navigation.
  • "Grid of squares" portfolios with zero business context.
  • Generic templates that do not match your business operations.

Frequently Asked Questions About Website Revamps

Do I need a visual redesign or an SEO revamp?

If your website looks bad, it hurts trust. But if your website has no SEO structure, nobody will ever see it to judge the design. A revamp fixes the underlying structure before applying a premium visual layer, ensuring you get both traffic and trust.

Why is my website getting traffic but no leads?

Usually, this means your "proof" (galleries, testimonials) is disconnected from your "capture" (contact forms, WhatsApp links). If a user has to click 3 times to find out how to message you after seeing a photo they like, they will leave.

I just need a catalog connected to WhatsApp — can you do that?

Yes. For many Instagram-first visual businesses, a full e-commerce checkout can add friction. We can design a premium catalog-to-WhatsApp path when that matches how buyers prefer to enquire.

How long does an SEO revamp take?

Most SEO-first revamps take 4 to 8 weeks depending on the number of pages, content readiness, and whether we are building new service or location pages. We start with a 1-week audit, written roadmap, and review call before any build work begins.

How much does an SEO website revamp cost in India?

Our SEO-first revamps typically range between ₹30,000 and ₹1,50,000 depending on the number of pages, content depth, and whether e-commerce or booking systems are involved. Every qualified project starts with a free audit: a written roadmap plus a review call within one week.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

Our core focus is India-based visual businesses — wedding planners, photographers, event studios, and bridal brands. If your business model and audience overlap with this, we are open to working with you regardless of location.

What is the difference between SEO and a website redesign?

A redesign changes the visual look — fonts, colors, layout. An SEO revamp restructures the pages, headings, metadata, local intent signals, and enquiry paths so that Google can rank your site and buyers can find what they need. Ideally, you do both — but structure should come first.

Will my website look different after an SEO revamp?

Yes. An SEO-first revamp includes a premium visual redesign. The difference is that we build the structure and content first, then apply a design that supports the SEO goals rather than fighting them.
